About Me

Meet Vans_Cmkro

Vanessa Camacaro, also known as Vans_Cmkro and Moms Talk Crypto, is a Digital-AI Artist, AI Engineer Student,

Photographer, Storyteller, Web3 Event Planner, and Curator. Originally from Venezuela and raised in South Florida,

she currently resides in Denver.


Vanessa is dedicated to supporting women in tech through her Women In Tech Museum, providing showcases, mentorship, and resources.

Her artwork has been showcased at prestigious events and institutions globally, including Art Basel Miami, NFT NYC, AI Fashion Week.

Exhibitions in Los Angeles, New York, South Carolina, Estonia, France, Rome, Canada, Portugal, Spain, Germany and Romania.
